Result Pillar Impact Summary
Result 1: Disaster Risk Management
CVA deployed across 11 National Societies as a preparedness and response tool, supporting contingency planning, anticipatory action, and emergency cash distributions.
Result 2: Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness and Response
No confirmed CVA delivery under this result pillar. Epidemic preparedness activities were carried out through non-cash modalities.
Result 3: Humanitarian Assistance and Protection for People on the Move
CVA integrated with protection services across 11 National Societies, providing flexible assistance to migrants, refugees, and displaced persons.
Result 5: Risk Communication, Community Engagement and Accountability
CVA institutionalised as a core competency across 14 National Societies through SOPs, roadmaps, FSP agreements, CWG leadership, and digital tools.
